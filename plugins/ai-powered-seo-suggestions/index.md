This documentation will help you use AI-Powered SEO Suggestions, a WordPress plugin that uses OpenAI to analyze your content (title, meta description, headings, body, links, and images) and surface clear, focused actions to improve discoverability and clicks. It also provides **AI Semantic Keyword Hints** to expand topical coverage and **AI Fix-It Tips** to turn scores into next steps.

For help learning how to install the plugin, add your details and display them on your website, visit the [User Guide](user).
