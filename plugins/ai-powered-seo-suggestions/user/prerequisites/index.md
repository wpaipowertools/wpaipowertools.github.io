title: Prerequisite Third-Party SEO Plugin
---
In order to effectively use the **AI-Powered SEO Suggestions** plugin, you must have installed and activated a third-party SEO plugin. **AI-Powered SEO Suggestions** pulls the info necessary to generate its suggestions and correctly implement its other functions from the data included/generated by the third-party SEO plugin of your choice. 

**AI-Powered SEO Suggestions** currently integrates with the following third-party SEO plugins:

- All-In-One SEO
- Yoast SEO
- Rank Math
- SEOPress 

You must install and activate at least one of the above plugins. To select which you would like to integrate with, go to **Settings > General > Compatibility > Integrations**.
