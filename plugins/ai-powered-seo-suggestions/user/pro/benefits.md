title: Benefits of the Pro Version

The pro version of the [WordPress AI-Powered SEO Plugin](https://wpaiplugins.dev/wordpress-ai-powered-seo-plugin/) includes several advanced features, such as custom controls, expanded semantic keywords, WooCommerce integration, a structured data assistant, exports and more.

Pro features include:

- **Custom Controls**: Customize the generation by setting the tone, target audience, brand keywords, temperature and more.
- **Semantic Keywords**: Discover related terms to improve coverage, including 10–20+ terms, clustering and “where to use” tips.
- **Schema Assistant**: Automatically generate and insert structured data for articles, products, FAQs and more.
- **WooCommerce**: Get product-specific SEO tips and fields, with the ability to set the schema type and generate meta.
- **Reports and History**: Get access to reports and trends, exports, and REST endpoints for automation.
- **Bulk Tools**: Speed up your workflow with batch analysis, quick fixes, scheduled re-scoring, CSV exports and more.
- **Stay up to date**: Immediate access to all security and bug fix updates as well as to all new features that get added.
- **Priority support**: Priority support for pro users, with quick and effective help from a real person.
